Who am I?

Fast food websites started in the 1990s with basic menus and store info. In the 2000s, online ordering and promotions became popular. By the 2010s, mobile-friendly sites and social media integration allowed easier ordering and delivery tracking. Today, these websites provide a full digital experience, helping customers order, pay, and engage with the brand online.
